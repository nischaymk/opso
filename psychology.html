Psychology Research Experts

Ms. Vaishnavi Joshi

Clinical Psychologist

Vaishnavi Joshi is an RCI Licensed Clinical Psychologist with extensive clinical, academic, and research experience in mental health. She holds an M.Phil. in Clinical Psychology from Mahatma Jyoti Rao Phule University and an M.Sc. in Clinical Psychology from Christ (Deemed to be) University. With over 400 hours of direct therapeutic work, she has provided psychological assessment and psychotherapy services across diverse populations.

Psychology Research Areas

Cognitive Psychology

Study of mental processes including perception, memory, attention, and problem-solving.

  • Memory and Learning
  • Attention and Perception
  • Decision Making
  • Language Processing

Clinical Psychology

Assessment, diagnosis, and treatment of mental health disorders and psychological distress.

  • Therapeutic Interventions
  • Psychological Assessment
  • Mental Health Disorders
  • Counseling Techniques

Social Psychology

Examination of how individuals think, feel, and behave in social contexts and groups.

  • Social Cognition
  • Group Dynamics
  • Interpersonal Relationships
  • Attitudes and Persuasion
